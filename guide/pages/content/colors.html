Colors

Theme colors

All these colors are managed in the $theme SASS variable.

.color-bg-green-dark
.color-bg-green-dark-alt
.color-bg-green-light
.color-bg-blue-dark
.color-bg-yellow-light
.color-bg-orange-dark
.color-bg-red-dark

As foreground colors:

.color-green-dark
.color-green-dark-alt
.color-green-light
.color-blue-dark
.color-yellow-light
.color-orange-dark
.color-red-dark

Grays

All these colors are managed in the $grays SASS variable.

.color-bg-gray-darker
.color-bg-gray-dark
.color-bg-gray-medium
.color-bg-gray-medium-light
.color-bg-gray-light
.color-bg-gray-lighter
.color-bg-gray-lightest

As foreground colors:

.color-gray-darker
.color-gray-dark
.color-gray-medium
.color-gray-medium-light
.color-gray-light
.color-gray-lighter
.color-gray-lightest

Black and white

Black and white are stored as vairables, $color-black and $color-white, in case there is a need to use slightly off values.

.
.color-bg-white
.color-bg-black

As foreground colors:

.
.color-white
.color-black
